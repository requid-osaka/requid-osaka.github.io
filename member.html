Requid Member Profiles

Project Members

Meet the members who lead and support Requid's responsible research and innovation activities in quantum technologies.

Sho Morishita

Research Center for ELSI, the University of Osaka

Sho Morishita, an associate professor (lecturer) at Research Center on Ethical, Legal, and Social issues at the University of Osaka, is an anthropologist of science and technology. He has conducted fieldwork and collaborative research across a wide range of scientific disciplines, including earth sciences, Indonesian astronomy, synthetic biology, primatology, and quantum science. He has been engaged in RRI research since 2020 and currently serves as the project leader for RRI in quantum science.

Konomi Higo

Research Center for ELSI, the University of Osaka

Konomi Higo, an assisstant professor at Research Center on Ethical, Legal, and Social issues at the University of Osaka. She specializes in Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI) of emerging sciences and technologies, with a focus on outreach, public engagement, and the communication of academic knowledge to broader audiences, including collaboration with industry and other stakeholders.

Yusuke Nagato

Research Center for ELSI, the University of Osaka

Yusuke Nagato, an associate professor (lecturer) at Research Center on Ethical, Legal, and Social issues at the University of Osaka. His research explores the intersection of quantum science and pseudoscience, with a particular focus on investment and policy. He has recently published a paper examining the ethical implications of "hype" within the field of quantum innovation.
