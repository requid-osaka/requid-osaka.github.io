RRI in Quantum Technologies

Designing a harmonious relationship between quantum technology and society.

Requid (Responsible Innovation and Development) is a group centered around researchers in the humanities and social sciences at the University of Osaka in Japan, dedicated to the study and practice of “Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI)” in quantum technologies. We connect researchers, practitioners, and communities to promote the development of ethical, inclusive, and just quantum technologies.

Contact Requid

Research

We study governance, ethics, and societal implications of emerging quantum technologies across policy and practice.

Practice

In collaboration with quantum experts, we are working on projects to design the future of society and developing serious games to promote RRI in quantum technologies.

Engagement

In preparation for a nationwide public dialogue on quantum technology to be held in the near future, we are launching pilot initiatives to engage in dialogue with non-experts.
